ABOUT

PSM (Persatuan Sindikat Menyontek) is a group formed with a spirit of togetherness and collaboration. Even though the name sounds naughty, the main activity of PSM is studying together in the campus library. Members of this circle often gather to discuss lecture material, do assignments, and share study tips.

Apart from studying, PSM is also a place for members to support each other, share stories and even joke to reduce stress. For them, the library is not only a place to stay quiet, but also a place to grow together as students who support each other.
